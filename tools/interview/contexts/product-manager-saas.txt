ROLE CONTEXT:
- Senior Product Manager / Group Product Manager
- B2B SaaS, post-product-market-fit, $50M-$1B ARR scale
- Owns a product surface or capability (e.g., billing, analytics, onboarding, integrations)

- "I gathered requirements" — passive language, no judgment shown
